## Project Introduction

**Game Introduction:**

*Starfield* is an open-world science-fiction RPG developed by Bethesda Game Studios. Built with the official Creation Kit, this project adds an original side quest that can be played independently within the game.

**Level Introduction:**

*Ghost Ship* is a single-player side-quest level designed for *Starfield*. After running out of fuel, the player boards a missing luxury starliner. During exploration, the player gradually restores the ship’s power, breaks into sealed areas, collects critical resources, and uses environmental storytelling to uncover the truth behind the ship’s destruction, ultimately confronting an unknown creature in the cargo hold. The level combines exploration, combat, stealth, puzzles, and environmental storytelling to create a suspenseful horror experience inspired by *Alien*.

## My Work

### 1. Level Layout and Progression

I designed the complete level flow, connecting the bridge, passenger cabins, crew areas, activity spaces, cargo hold, and other functional zones into a coherent exploration route. Restoring power, collecting keys, and solving battery puzzles gradually unlock new areas and create a layered exploration rhythm.

### 2. Environmental Storytelling

I built the world of an abandoned luxury starliner and used corpse placement, terminal logs, scene details, lighting changes, and damaged spaces to help players gradually infer the truth of the incident while creating an *Alien*-style suspense-horror atmosphere.

### 3. Exploration, Puzzle, and Combat Design

I designed exploration and puzzle gameplay around restoring power, searching for keys, collecting batteries, crawling through ventilation shafts, and discovering secret rooms. Xenogrubs, Terrormorphs, and other enemies make exploration, puzzles, and combat alternate throughout the experience and continuously increase tension.

### 4. Pacing Design

A dark zero-gravity opening, power restoration, free exploration, a confined chase, resource management, and the final boss fight form a complete pacing arc that gradually escalates from exploration to climactic combat.
