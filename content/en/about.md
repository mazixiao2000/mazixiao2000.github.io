## How I Design Levels

I begin by defining what the player should **understand, decide, and feel** at each stage. I then organize routes, scale, sightlines, enemies, interactions, and environmental information into a testable experience. My projects typically begin with flow diagrams and blockouts, move through repeated playtests for readability and pacing, and are refined in collaboration with programmers and artists.

:::callout title="Design Principle"
A strong level should allow players to understand their current objective, perceive the structure of the space, and learn rules through the results of their own actions without relying on lengthy instruction.
:::
