## Design Focus

I focus on what players need to **understand, decide, and feel** as they move through a space. My work connects routes, combat, mechanics, narrative, and visual information to a clear experiential goal rather than treating them as isolated elements.

:::capabilities
- Space & Flow | Build readable player paths through spatial hierarchy, route structure, and purposeful revisitation.
- Combat & Mechanics | Integrate enemy composition, cover, sightlines, movement, and core mechanics into legible encounters.
- Prototyping & Implementation | Independently create playable work in Creation Kit, Hammer, Unreal Engine, and Unity.
- Collaboration & Iteration | Experienced in a 42-person Scrum team, a four-person team, and solo projects with playtest-driven feedback loops.
:::
