I am Zixiao Ma, an M.I.T. candidate in Digital Game Development on the Level Design track at SMU Guildhall. I earned my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang Sci-Tech University and previously worked as a Systems Design Intern at NetEase Games, contributing to system design, content configuration, feature testing, and cross-team development for the overseas version of Revelation Mobile.

How I Design Levels#

I begin by defining what players should understand, decide, and feel in each phase, then organize routes, scale, sightlines, enemies, interactions, and environmental information into a testable experience. My projects typically move from paper flow and blockout to repeated playtests for readability, pacing, and gameplay variation, followed by implementation with programmers and artists.

Design Principle

A level should let players understand their current goal, read the spatial structure, and learn from the consequences of their actions without relying on lengthy explanation.
