## Design Approach

I focus on what players need to **understand, decide, and feel** at each stage of an experience. My work goes beyond constructing spaces: routes, encounters, mechanics, narrative, and visual information must all support a clear experiential goal.

:::capabilities
- Space & Flow | Build readable player paths through spatial hierarchy, route structure, and purposeful reuse.
- Combat & Mechanics | Integrate enemy composition, cover, sightlines, movement, and core mechanics into legible encounters.
- Prototyping & Implementation | Create playable prototypes independently in Creation Kit, Hammer, Unreal Engine, and Unity.
- Collaboration & Iteration | Experienced in a 42-person Scrum team, four-person teams, and solo projects, with a strong playtest-feedback loop.
:::
